### mechanical waves
* can be transverse 
	* s-waves
	* stadium wave
	* particles move perpendicular to wave direction
* or longitudinal (compression)
	* sound
	* p-wave
	* particles move parallel to the direction of motion
### electromagnetic waves
* no need for a medium
* can only be transverse
* light, x-ray, gamma
