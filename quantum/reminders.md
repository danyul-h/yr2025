$$
\displaylines{
\text{metric prefixes:} \\
\begin{eqnarray}
m = milli &=& 10^{-3} \\
\mu = micro &=& 10^{-6} \\
n = nano &=& 10^{-9} \\
p = pico &=& 10^{-12}
\end{eqnarray}
}
$$

mass (kg) != weight (newtons)
* an object will have a mass of 8.17kg, it does not weigh 8.17kg
* weight = mg
* mass = m
